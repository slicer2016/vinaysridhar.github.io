Tabula Rasa

John McPhee - a prolific writer I love - started a series called Tabula Rasa, an "anthology of writing projects McPhee couldnâ€™t get around to, subjects he wanted to explore but never did, not at any length."

I love that idea. While I sometimes devote the energy to write an essay, more often, I come across interesting ideas that never result in a fully formed POV. This page is a running list of these ideas -- from podcasts, articles, videos and tweets.

I prefer this to tweeting because I think tweeting invites often unhelpful, low-quality discussion (I deleted my Twitter in 2023). I found myself spending more energy in responding to a random opinion (xkcd captures this well) versus substantive engagement. That said, I did find value in logging ideas - and this is a step in that direction. To make this easier, I built a Telegram bot that allows me to text a link with a quick take that is then published below. It's made the whole process pretty smooth.
