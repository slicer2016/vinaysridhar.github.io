Tabula Rasa

John McPhee - a fantastic and inspiring writer - started a series called Tabula Rasa where he "compiles 50 bits of prose, from a single paragraph to 10 or more pages, that McPhee never published or, in many cases, even completed".

I love that idea. I come across things that really make me think -- and prompts the time and energy investment of writing an essay. Often, however, I come across mildly interesting ideas that never form a fully formed POV. This is a running list of things I'm consuming and thinking about.
