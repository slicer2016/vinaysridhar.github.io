Book Reviews

This is a collection of some of my book reviews. Most of my non-fiction reading is about art, culture, history, india, memoirs, mental health and science/nature. I also read 10-15 novels a year, but do not review them. These reviews were written right after I completed each book and capture what I thought then, versus what I think about these subjects today.

I also subscribe to Teju's Cole idea that the distinction between fiction and non-fiction is an arbitrary construction, but hey - it does have its uses.
