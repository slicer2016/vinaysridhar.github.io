About Me

I grew up in Bangalore and now live in Brooklyn with my wife, dog and daughter. I am currently a Product Manager at Reddit. Over the years, I have worked in media, consulting and government in India and the US. I studied ocean engineering at IIT Madras and business at Harvard University. See my Linkedin for more details on my professional life.
